PAYMENT HELP

Price updates We occasionally update our prices as we continue to innovate and invest in giving you better listening experiences and more value than ever before. We may also adjust our prices to reflect local factors and meet market demands while offering an unparalleled service to listeners.
We're always working to bring you a highly personalized experience and powerful discovery tools.
Is my plan price changing?
If your plan's price is changing, we'll email you in advance with information about the new price and when the change will happen.
You can check your billing details any time on your account page.
What other plans are available?
We have a number of Premium plans that you can check out at www.spotify.com/premium.
You can also cancel your Premium plan to use our free, ad-supported service instead. You'll keep access to your favorite music and podcasts plus all your saved tracks, playlists, and podcasts.

Cancel Premium plans Cancel your Premium plan any time on your account page.
Log into spotify.com/account.
Under Manage your plan, click Change plan.
Scroll to Cancel Spotify and click Cancel Premium.
Your Premium stays until your next billing date, then your account switches to free.
You keep your playlists and saved music when your account is free. You can also still log in and play with ads.
Want to cancel your Spotify Family or Duo plan?
If you're a plan manager, follow the steps above to cancel your plan. Make sure you let all plan members know as they'll lose their Premium benefits starting from the next payment date.
If you're a plan member, following the steps above will remove your account from the plan, but won't cancel it. Reach out to the plan manager to cancel or update the payment method.
Check out our guide if you want to switch Premium plans instead.
